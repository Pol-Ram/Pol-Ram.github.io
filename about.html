Awards and Recognitions

Bronze Medal at Robomatrix Robotics Competition 2021

In the Robot Dance category of the Latin American regional robotics competition, I developed a choreography for the Nao humanoid robot, programming in Python a sequence of movements synchronized with music. The project required using specific libraries from the Naoqi SDK for joint control, balance, and precise timing.

I added a creative touch with a Mazinger Z costume

Second Place at Continental HackToMove 2024

During the Continental Hackathon, we proposed an innovative solution to monetize parked vehicles in urban environments. The idea involved implementing a passive blockchain system, using the unused computational capacity of vehicles' electronic control units (ECUs) to contribute to digital certificate validation and banking transactions.
